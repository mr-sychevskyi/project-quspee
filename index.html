FAQ

How much tasks I can provide?

You can give as many tasks as you need, you have no limit or restrictions. They will be distributed in time and executed as soon as possible (1-3 days depending on the task).

Who is your service for?

Our service is designed for anyone who needs a good quality design for a small payment, we do not do a brilliant and super creative design, we do a good job for a good price, it is suitable for everyone, start-ups, agencies, freelancers, bloggers, photographers, existing businesses etc.

How does your service work in detail?

First you choose the services you need, then pay the bill, we will send you a brief in which you describe everything and we start to perform, specifying the details simultaneously, then show you the result, you see if you need to fix something - we do it, so much as long as you need, that you would be satisfied and then give you all the materials.

Can I get my money back if I do not like the designs?

Yes, you will have 7 days for a refund. If you have taken the source files, then we define it as an accepted project.

If I already have prototype, can you work with it?

Of course, we can earn from scratch or use your prototypes to create a design. If there is no prototype, you need to do it initially, then go to the implementation of the design.

Do you give the source files?

Yes, at the end of the work you get all the necessary materials.

How many changes can I make?

You can make edits until you are completely satisfied with the result!
